About Us:



The Cultural Committee strives to celebrate the cultural diversity on the campus by organizing pooja/cultural performances/specials dinners/decorating college during various festivals and events. The committee ensures that the students have a home-away-from-home experience. The committee provides enough opportunities for the students to relax and enjoy campus life amidst rigorous academics.


The student community here comes from diversified backgrounds, and celebrating our differences, as well as our common interests, helps us unite and learn from each other. We understand the importance of cultural sensitivity in today's business world and the cultural committee makes the young leaders sensitive to each other's culture.
